Rezolve - Order Orchestration & Fulfillment Documentation
=========================================================

Welcome to Rezolve, the platform that powers both **high-precision location services** and **order orchestration & fulfillment** experiences. Whether you're building curbside pickup, drive-thru optimization, location-based marketing, or real-time customer arrival notifications, Rezolve provides the tools you need.

## What You Can Build

### Order Fulfillment & Operations
- **Curbside Pickup**: Notify staff when customers arrive with real-time ETA predictions
- **Drive-Thru Optimization**: Fire orders at the perfect time based on customer location
- **In-Store Pickup**: Streamline pickup experiences with arrival notifications
- **Delivery Tracking**: Monitor driver locations and provide accurate ETAs

### Location-Based Experiences
- **Proximity Marketing**: Trigger campaigns when customers enter geofenced areas
- **Customer Journey Tracking**: Understand how customers move through physical spaces
- **Gamification**: Create location-based games and challenges
- **Analytics & Insights**: Generate reports on customer behavior and location patterns
